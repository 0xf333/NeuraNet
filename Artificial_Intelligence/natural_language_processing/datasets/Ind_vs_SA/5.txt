IND vs SA 2022 Match DatesThe South African cricket team will begin their tour of India with a T20 series on September 28th, 2022 at Greenfield International Stadium, Thiruvananthapuram, as shown in the India vs South Africa Time Table The second Twenty20 match will take place on October 2nd, 2022 at Barsapara Cricket Stadium, Guwahati The third and fourth T20 matches will be held on the 4th and 6th of October 2022, respectively, at Indore and Lucknow, and the final two T20 matches will be held on the 9th and 11th of October 2022 in Ranchi and Delhi Also see - IND vs SA Head to Head
