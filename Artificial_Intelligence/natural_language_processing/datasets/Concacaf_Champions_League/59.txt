(AP Photo/Laurence Kesterson)By Ben Axelson | baxelson@syracuse.comThe Philadelphia Union hosts LAFC at Subaru Park in Chester, Pennsylvania on Wednesday, April 26 (4/26/2023) at 9 p.m. ET for leg 1 of their 2023 CONCACAF Champions League semifinal.The game will be broadcast live on FS1, and can be streamed live on fuboTV (free trial) and other live TV services.Stream the CONCACAF Champions League on fuboTV (free trial).Philadelphia played Atlas to a 2-2 draw in the second leg of their quarterfinal matchup, but advanced on aggregate score thanks to a 1-0 win in the first leg. LAFC consistently dominated the Vancouver Whitecaps in the quarterfinals, winning back-to-back 3-0 games to advance.These two Major League Soccer rivals are among the best in the league, and now they’ll square off for CONCACAF cup glory. The winner advances to this year’s CCL final where they’ll play against either Liga MX’s Tigres UANL or Club León.Here’s how to tune in.What: CONCACAF Champions League semifinal, leg 1Who: LAFC @ Philadelphia UnionWhen: Wednesday, April 26Where: Subaru Park, Chester, PennsylvaniaTime: 9 p.m. ETTV: FS1Channel finder: Verizon Fios, XFinity, Spectrum, Optimum/Altice, Cox, DirecTVLive stream: fuboTV (free trial), Sling (half off first month), DirecTV Stream (free trial), Hulu + Live TVCord cutters can sign up for fuboTV (free trial), Sling (half off first month) or DirecTV Stream (free trial) to enjoy broadcasts of FS1.Cable subscribers can log in on FOXSports.com to enjoy a free live stream of the channel.MLS gear, including hats, t-shirts and jerseys for your favorite teams can be found on Fanatics.Can I bet on the game?Yes, you can bet on U.S. and international soccer from your phone in New York State, and we’ve compiled some of the best introductory offers to help navigate your first bets from BetMGM, FanDuel, DraftKings, PointsBet, Caesars and BetRivers.Philadelphia is +110 to win, according to DraftKings.What is fuboTV?FuboTV is a live TV streaming service focused on live sports, including U.S. and international soccer, the NFL, MLB, NBA, NHL and more.
