Product Description

Classic upholstered project chair gives the right design and maximum value. Practical and hard-working so it's great for office or school. Armless style works great at a task table and easy to pull up as extra seating. Steel tubular frame with bronze powder coat painted cover. 3" dual wheel carpet casters provide a smooth and quiet ride. Grade two fabric is tough and easy to clean Face forward. Weight capacity is 250 lbs. Seat measures 18"W x 16"D. Back measures 14-1/4"W x 13"H. Seat height is 18". Overall chair size is 19"W x 20-1/2"D x 31-1/2"H. Coordinating stools are available! Assembly required. Available in Blue and Red.
